When editing files under `wiki/`:
- preserve human readability first
- prefer short sections with strong links over long undifferentiated notes
- use Obsidian wikilinks for stable concepts and entities
- keep one idea per page where practical
- include `Source:` lines for factual claims derived from raw material
- if a page becomes too broad, split it and leave links behind
